  • 下载源代码: http://code.google.com/p/nutz/downloads/list

@Many 一对多映射 @see JDoc: org.nutz.dao.entity.annotation.Many

@One 一对一映射 @see JDoc: org.nutz.dao.entity.annotation.One

@View 视图名 @see JDoc: org.nutz.dao.entity.annotation.View
